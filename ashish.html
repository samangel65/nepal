Meet Mr. Ashish Shrestha – Drone Software Development Lead

We are excited to introduce Mr. Ashish Shrestha, a passionate and forward-thinking tech enthusiast who is now a vital part of the Drone Guru Pvt Ltd team.

Ashish holds a Master’s degree in Information Technology (Software Design and Development) from Charles Sturt University, Australia. With over 7 years of hands-on experience in the technology and drone industry—both in Nepal and abroad—Ashish brings a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and a deep passion for innovation.

His mission is to transform the way drones are utilized in Nepal by leading the development of cutting-edge drone software solutions tailored to local and global needs. At Drone Guru, Ashish is focused on blending smart software with modern drone technology to unlock new possibilities in fields like mapping, agriculture, surveillance, and training.

With his strong foundation in IT, real-world experience, and an innovative mindset, Ashish is committed to shaping the future of drones in Nepal—one line of code at a time.
