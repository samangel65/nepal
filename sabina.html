Sabina Karmacharya – Director of Innovation & Administration

Sabina Karmacharya is a dynamic and visionary businesswoman with a Master’s degree in Business Administration from the UK. Having lived in the UK for over 25 years, Sabina brings a wealth of international experience and a deep understanding of global business practices.

From a young age, Sabina displayed an exceptional entrepreneurial spirit, tailoring and launching innovative businesses across different sectors. Her passion for creativity and a strong drive for success have led her to build and grow ventures that stand out for their uniqueness and impact.

Now, as a key member of Drone Guru Pvt Ltd, Sabina is steering the organization into new heights by introducing fresh perspectives and innovative strategies. She plays a crucial role in managing all day-to-day administrative operations while ensuring the company stays aligned with its vision of transforming the drone industry in Nepal and beyond.

With a strong belief in the power of innovation, Sabina is committed to creating opportunities, empowering teams, and paving a new path forward for Drone Guru.
