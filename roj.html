Meet Mr. Roj Shrestha – Multimedia Technician

We are proud to introduce Mr. Roj Shrestha, a talented and passionate Multimedia Technician at Drone Guru Pvt Ltd.

Roj holds a Bachelor’s degree in Multimedia Technologies from London Metropolitan University, United Kingdom. With over 5 years of professional experience in the multimedia and drone industry, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic creativity to our team.

His deep passion for aerial photography and videography drives him to explore new angles, new heights, and new perspectives. At Drone Guru, Roj plays a key role in capturing stunning visual content that tells compelling stories from the skies.

He is now dedicated to tailoring a new era of aerial imaging in Nepal, combining cutting-edge drone technology with creative multimedia techniques. Roj’s vision and dedication are helping Drone Guru set new standards in aerial content creation for industries such as media, tourism, real estate, and more.
