Course Overview

The RePL course offered by Drone Guru Pvt Ltd is tailored for individuals and professionals in Nepal aiming to become certified drone pilots. This training meets the standards set by the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA) and covers both theoretical and practical components required to operate drones legally and safely.

What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to drones and airspace rules in Nepal
  • Drone operation and maintenance fundamentals
  • Flight planning and emergency procedures
  • Weather, safety, and risk management
  • Live flight practice with professional instructors

Course Duration

8 to 12 weeks (includes theory, practical training, and examination)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Basic English and mathematical skills
  • Valid citizenship or passport
  • Medically fit to operate a drone

Certification

After successful completion, you will receive a Remotely Piloted Aircraft Licence (RePL) issued under the guidelines of Drone Guru Private Limited.

Career Opportunities in Nepal

  • Aerial photography and videography
  • Real estate and infrastructure monitoring
  • Agricultural drone services (spraying & mapping)
  • Disaster response and search & rescue support
  • Environmental and wildlife monitoring and more
