Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AeRoC)

Offered by Drone Guru Pvt. Ltd.

Course Overview:

The Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AROC) is a crucial qualification for drone pilots operating in controlled airspace or areas requiring radio communication with Air Traffic Control (ATC). At Drone Guru Pvt. Ltd., we offer an AROC course tailored to the needs of modern drone operators, blending theoretical and practical knowledge to ensure safety, compliance, and professional communication in the air.

Course Highlights:

  • Understanding aviation communication protocols
  • Radio phraseology and standard procedures
  • Hands-on radio communication practice
  • Emergency communication procedures
  • Regulatory framework and compliance

Why is AROC Important for Drone Operators?

  • Legal Compliance: Ensures your operations are legal in controlled or regulated airspace.
  • Enhanced Safety: Clear communication minimizes risks and improves airspace coordination.
  • Professional Credibility: Boosts your qualifications and trust among clients and authorities.
  • More Opportunities: Enables BVLOS, night operations, and missions near airports or sensitive zones.

Who Should Enroll?

  • Aspiring professional drone pilots
  • Commercial drone operators
  • Drone instructors and trainers
  • Operators planning VLOS, BVLOS or controlled airspace flights
