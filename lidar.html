Lidar Mapping

What We Offer

1. Topographic Mapping & Surveying

  • Generate precise topographic maps and digital terrain models (DTMs) of land surfaces.
  • Create detailed elevation models to aid in land development, construction, and environmental analysis.
  • Efficiently cover large and difficult-to-reach areas, reducing the need for ground-based surveying.

2. Construction & Engineering Surveys

  • Use LiDAR to monitor construction sites and map out building structures with high accuracy.
  • Quickly collect data on existing conditions, elevations, and measurements for engineering purposes.
  • Ideal for building foundations, roadways, pipelines, and other infrastructure projects.

3. Forestry & Vegetation Analysis

  • Analyze forest canopy heights, tree density, and biomass with LiDAR's high-resolution data.
  • Assess vegetation health and environmental change over time.
  • LiDAR is highly effective in densely vegetated areas where traditional surveying methods may struggle.

4. Mining & Quarrying

  • Capture accurate 3D models of mine sites, quarries, and stockpiles.
  • Monitor volume changes and stockpile measurements for efficient resource management.
  • Ensure safety with real-time, accurate data for mining operations and site planning.

5. Flood Modeling & Hydrology

  • Generate precise floodplain models and simulate flood risks.
  • Map riverbeds, coastlines, and other hydrological features for better flood management.
  • Support environmental studies by providing data for drainage planning and infrastructure projects.

6. Coastal & Infrastructure Monitoring

  • Survey coastlines, beaches, and erosion-prone areas with high precision.
  • Monitor infrastructure, including bridges, dams, and roads, for structural changes over time.
  • Detect shifts or deformation of infrastructure, providing early warning for potential failures.

7. Archaeological & Heritage Site Surveys

  • Use LiDAR to map archaeological sites, uncover hidden structures, and monitor site preservation.
  • Non-intrusive data collection preserves delicate areas while providing comprehensive survey data.
  • Ideal for mapping large areas of historical sites and protected environments.
